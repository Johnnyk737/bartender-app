Baileys Vanilla Silk

400 2.5
Medium

A cocktail that brings together three deliciously simple elements that combine perfectly: sweet almonds, vanilla and creamy richness in Amaretto, vanilla vodka and Baileys Original Irish Cream Liqueur.

How to make

  1. Fill a shaker with ice.

    Fill a cocktail shaker with ice cubes.

  2. Add Baileys Original Irish Cream Liqueur, Smirnoff Vanilla Flavoured Vodka and amaretto.

    Using a jigger, measure 25ml Baileys Original Irish Cream Liqueur, 30ml Smirnoff Vanilla Flavoured Vodka and 10ml amaretto into the shaker.

  3. Shake until cold.

    Shake the mixture vigorously until the surface of the cocktail shaker feels chilled.

  4. Strain into a glass filled with ice.

    Using a cocktail strainer, strain into a tumbler filled with ice cubes.

Baileys® Original Irish Cream

Since its creation in 1974, Baileys Original Irish Cream Liqueur, a delicious blend of quality whiskey and smooth Irish cream, has become one of the world’s best-loved liqueurs.
