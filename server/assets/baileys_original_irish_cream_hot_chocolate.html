Baileys Original Irish Cream Hot Chocolate

621 3
Medium

A delicious variation on the classic hot chocolate drink, this adds a fabulous rich, creamy dimension that's not hard to create but made to be savoured with a few friends.

1
▸
  • 50ml.
    Baileys® Original Irish Cream Liqueur
  • 150ml.
    Hot Chocolate
  • 1piece(s)
    Whipped Cream
  • 1shaving(s)
    Chocolate Shaving
  • ➤ 0.8 units of alcohol per serve

Equipment

  • 1 x
    Short Glass
  • 1 x
    Jigger
  • 1 x
    Knife

How to make

  1. Pour Baileys Original Irish Cream Liqueur and hot chocolate into a glass.

    Using a jigger, measure 150ml hot chocolate into a saucepan and heat gently. Pour into a short glass with 50ml of Baileys Original Irish Cream Liqueur.

  2. Garnish with whipped cream and chocolate curls.

    Squirt whipped cream onto the drink and sprinkle chocolate curls on top of the cream.

Avoid top-ups

It’s easier to keep track of your drinks if you know how many full glasses you’re drinking.

Baileys® Original Irish Cream

Since its creation in 1974, Baileys Original Irish Cream Liqueur, a delicious blend of quality whiskey and smooth Irish cream, has become one of the world’s best-loved liqueurs.
