Apple Bite Gold

537 3.5
Easy

A long drink that blends the delicate cinnamon flavour in Smirnoff Gold with apple – a natural flavour partner. This is a fine combination of tastes that’s lifted by effervescent citrus lemonade and the theatre of real edible gold flakes.

Ingredients for

◄
1
▸
  • 50ml.
    Smirnoff® Gold
  • 100ml.
    Lemonade
  • 100ml.
    Apple Juice
  • 1piece(s)
    Lime Wedge
  • ➤ 1.9 units of alcohol per serve

Equipment

  • 1 x
    Tall glass
  • 1 x
    Ice
  • 1 x
    Jigger

How to make

  1. Fill a glass with ice.

    Fill a tall glass with ice cubes.

  2. Add equal parts lemonade and apple juice

  3. Add Smirnoff Gold

    Add the Smirnoff Gold at the end to dramatize the effect of the gold flakes falling through the drink.

  4. Garnish with a wedge of lime

    Squeeze in a wedge of lime and drop in drink to garnish.

Avoid top-ups

It’s easier to keep track of your drinks if you know how many full glasses you’re drinking.

Smirnoff® Gold

A striking version of Smirnoff, with sparkling gold flakes, Smirnoff Gold is the epitome of luxury, with a great, subtle cinnamon spice taste.
