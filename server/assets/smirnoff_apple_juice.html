Smirnoff & Apple Juice

570 2.5
Easy

A simple and tasty aperitif, Smirnoff No. 21 Vodka and apple juice is an elegant, tart mixed drink that can be garnished with a slice of apple for extra effect.

Ingredients for

◄
1
▸
  • 50ml.
    Smirnoff No. 21® Vodka
  • 125ml.
    Apple Juice
  • 1slice(s)
    Apple
  • ➤ 1.9 units of alcohol per serve

Equipment

  • 1 x
    Tall glass
  • 1 x
    Knife
  • 1 x
    Jigger
  • 1 x
    Chopping Board
  • 1 x
    Ice

How to make

  1. Pour Smirnoff No. 21 Vodka and apple juice over ice cubes in a tall glass.

    Use a jigger to measure 50ml Smirnoff No. 21 Vodka and 125ml apple juice. Pour into a tall glass over ice cubes.

  2. Garnish with a slice of apple.

    Using a sharp knife cut a slice of apple on a chopping board, drop into glass and serve.

Avoid top-ups

It’s easier to keep track of your drinks if you know how many full glasses you’re drinking.

Smirnoff No. 21® Vodka

A vodka that's known around the world, Smirnoff is born of a long history of charcoal filtration to give smooth mouth feel and a pure, clean flavour.
