Don Julio Blanco Tequila & Cola

731 3
Easy

An unusual combination, but truly a delicious one, serve Don Julio Blanco Tequila and cola with a squeeze of lime for a cool, refreshing mixed drink that your guests will love.

How to make

  1. Fill a glass with ice.

    Fill a tall glass with ice cubes.

  2. Pour Don Julio Blanco Tequila and cola into the glass.

    Using a jigger, measure 50ml Don Julio Blanco Tequila and 125ml cola into the glass.

  3. Garnish with a lime wedge.

    Cut a wedge of lime with a sharp knife on a chopping board and place on top of the drink to garnish.

Don Julio® Blanco Tequila
