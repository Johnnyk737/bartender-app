Rob Roy

288 2
Medium

Created in 1894 by a bartender at the Waldorf-Astoria Hotel in New York City, the Rob Roy is a mix of Scotch whisky and vermouth that delivers a burst of sweet, earthy and spicy flavours.

How to make

  1. Fill a glass with ice.

    Fill a mixing glass with ice cubes.

  2. Place The Singleton of Dufftown Malt Whisky and sweet vermouth into the glass.

    Using a jigger, measure 50ml The Singleton of Dufftown Malt Whisky and 25ml sweet vermouth into the glass.

  3. Stir the mixture until well combined.

    Stir the mixture using a bar spoon.

  4. Strain into a glass.

    Using a strainer, strain into a martini glass.

  5. Garnish with a cherry.

    Place one cherry on top of the drink to garnish.
