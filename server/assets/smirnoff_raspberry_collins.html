Smirnoff Raspberry Collins

246 2.5
Medium

A light and fruity blend of Smirnoff No. 21 Vodka, fresh lemon juice, sweet sugar and muddled strawberries, this impressive-looking drink is ideal for summer parties with friends.

How to make

  1. Muddle raspberries in the base of a shaker.

    Muddle 8 raspberries in the base of a shaker.

  2. Fill a shaker with ice.

    Fill a cocktail shaker with ice cubes.

  3. Pour Smirnoff No. 21 Vodka, lemon juice and sugar syrup into the shaker.

    Using a jigger, measure 50ml Smirnoff No. 21 Vodka, 25ml lemon juice and 10ml sugar syrup into the shaker.

  4. Shake until cold.

    Shake the mixture vigorously until the surface of the cocktail shaker feels chilled.

  5. Strain into a Collins glass filled with ice.

    Strain the mixture into a Collins glass filled with ice with a cocktail strainer.

  6. Top up with carbonated water and garnish with raspberry.

    Top up with carbonated water and place 1 raspberry on top of the drink to garnish.
