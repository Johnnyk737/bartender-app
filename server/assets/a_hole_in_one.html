A Hole in One

208 2
Medium

This is a Manhattan plus tasty lemon juice and orange bitters, a citrus fruit lift given to the classic cocktail using the mixable Johnnie Walker Red Label to impress your guests.

How to make

  1. Fill a shaker with ice...

    Fill a cocktail shaker with ice cubes.

  2. Add Johnnie Walker Red Label Whisky, dry vermouth, lemon juice and orange bitters into the shaker.

    Using a jigger, pour 25ml Johnnie Walker Red Label Whisky, 15ml dry vermouth into the shaker. Add a dash of lemon juice and a dash of orange bitters.

  3. Shake until cold.

    Shake the mixture vigorously until the surface of the cocktail shaker feels chilled.

  4. Strain into a glass.

    Using a cocktail strainer, strain into a cocktail glass.
