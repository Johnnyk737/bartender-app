Tequila Pina Colada

360 3
Medium

A play on the ultimate Tiki drink, the Tequila Pina Colada replaces rum with tequila, but keeps the pineapple and coconut for a burst of flavour that’s richly evocative and tropical in feel.

How to make

  1. Pour Don Julio Blanco Tequila, coconut cream and pineapple juice into a blender.

    Using a jigger, measure 50ml Don Julio Blanco Tequila, 50ml coconut cream and 110ml pineapple juice into a blender.

  2. Blend together.

    Blend the ingredients together until smooth.

  3. Half-fill a glass with ice.

    Half-fill a Snifter glass with ice cubes.

  4. Pour the mixture into the glass.

    Pour the mixture from the blender into the glass.

  5. Garnish with a slice of pineapple.

    Cut a slice of pineapple with a sharp knife on a cutting board and slot over the side of the glass to garnish.
