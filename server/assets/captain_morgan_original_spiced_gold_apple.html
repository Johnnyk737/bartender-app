Captain Morgan Original Spiced Gold Apple

467 3
Easy

A delicious take on the autumnal dish of spiced apple that takes Captain Morgan Original Spiced Gold, adds a pour of apple juice and ends up with a great long drink.

How to make

  1. Fill a glass with ice.

    Fill a tall glass with ice cubes.

  2. Pour Captain Morgan Original Spiced Gold and apple juice into the glass.

    Using a jigger, measure 50ml Captain Morgan Original Spiced Gold and 125ml apple juice into the glass.

  3. Garnish with an apple slice or twist of apple peel.

    With a sharp knife and chopping board, cut a slice of apple and place on top of the drink to garnish.

Grab a bite

Get some grub in before or while you’re drinking – it slows alcohol absorption.
