Perfect Singapore Sling

452 2
Advanced

Singapore became synonymous with this iconic drink's rising popularity in the early 1900s and has continued ever since especially when using Tanqueray London Dry Gin.

How to make

  1. Add Tanqueray London Dry Gin, cherry brandy, benedictine, lemon juice and grenadine to a cocktail shaker filled with ice cubes and shake until cold.

    Using a jigger, measure 25ml of Tanqueray London Dry Gin, 25ml cherry brandy, 5ml of benedictine, 25ml of lemon juice and 10ml of grenadine. Pour into a cocktail shaker filled with ice cubes and shake until cold.

  2. Strain into a Collins glass filled with ice cubes and top with soda water.

    With a cocktail strainer, strain the liquid into a Collins glass and top with a splash of soda water.
