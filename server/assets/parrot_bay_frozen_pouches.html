Parrot Bay Frozen Pouches

These ready-to-drink frozen cocktails bring a taste of the Caribbean into your home. There’s the zesty Margarita, the sweetness of summer Berry Daiquiri, the juicy Passion Fruit Caipirinha and the tantalisingly tropical taste of Piña Colada and Cuban Mojito.

How to Enjoy

Freeze, squeeze and enjoy. These are the three steps to a great taste of Parrot Bay because these easy-to-pour pouches are made to be easily served. First place Parrot Bay pouches in the freezer at least eight hours before serving. Then, when you want to share with friends or have a tasty serve yourself, take the pouch out and squeeze until you feel it turning to slush (or run it quickly under a hot tap). Finally pour your Parrot Bay pouches into a glass while they're still partly frozen – and share.

Flavours

Parrot Bay is bursting with lush flavours. When combined with the tantalising soft crushed ice texture they give you the classic cocktail with the ultimate refreshing taste! So join in on the fun at ‘the Bay’ and make everyday moments with friends even more enjoyable with Parrot Bay Frozen Cocktails.

The Range

Choose from five great flavours: Parrot Bay Margarita, Parrot Bay Berry Daiquiri, Parrot Bay Mojito, Parrot Bay Passion Fruit Caipirinha and Parrot Bay Piña Colada. The Parrot Bay range of Frozen Cocktails offers ideal party drinks with a range of flavours to choose from. It's perfect for when people arrive unannounced – just take a few pouches from the freezer. And you can keep a few back to serve up a taste of sunshine in a glass any time – even in winter.

Grab a bite

Get some grub in before or while you’re drinking – it slows alcohol absorption.
