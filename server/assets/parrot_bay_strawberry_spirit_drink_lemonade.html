About this recipe

Bring together Parrot Bay Strawberry Spirit Drink & Lemonade for a truly tasty mixed drink. Delicious because Parrot Bay Sprit Drinks have been created with the mixers we have at home in mind, especially cola and lemonade. It’s the perfect drink for friends or relaxing. You might be enjoying a night in, or you could be throwing a house party with a few friends round. Whatever your plans, Parrot Bay Strawberry Spirit Drink & Lemonade is easy to make and delicious. Make your Parrot Bay Strawberry Spirit Drink with a handful of ice added to a glass. Then pour in a 50ml measure of Parrot Bay Strawberry Spirit Drink, top with 200ml lemonade, cut half a strawberry, drop in and serve. It’s not only delicious, but also so simple. The subtle citrus edge of lemonade lengthens the strawberry flavour, and there’s extra juicy fruit from the strawberry garnish. Try out two other great flavours in the Parrot Bay Spirit Drink range: Coconut and Passion Fruit. These great flavours are ready to go with your favourite mixers – Parrot Bay Coconut Spirit Drink & Cola and Parrot Bay Passion Fruit Spirit Drink & Lemonade. The Parrot Bay Spirit Drink range is colourful and with a lower ABV. Plus there’s more with the Parrot Bay Freeze and Squeeze cocktail pouches range. If you enjoyed Parrot Bay Spirit Drinks, you’ll love this selection of Berry Daiquiri, Passion Fruit Caipirinha, Pina Colada and Mojito, Margarita.

How to make

  1. Fill a glass with ice.

    Add cubes of ice to a tumbler or Collins glass.

  2. Pour Parrot Bay Strawberry Spirit Drink and lemonade into the glass.

    Using a jigger, measure 50ml Parrot Bay Strawberry Spirit Drink and 200ml lemonade into the glass.

  3. Garnish with strawberry.

    With a sharp knife and chopping board, cut half a strawberry and place on top of the drink to garnish.

Parrot Bay® Strawberry

Parrot Bay Spirit Drinks are perfect for when you have friends around. Try subtly sweet and juicy Strawberry flavour, zingy Passion Fruit or mouth-watering Coconut. You can keep it simple, Caribbean-style, by mixing with lemonade or cola. Serve over cubes of clinking ice in your tumbler, or mix in a long glass for a delicately sweet and fruity drink that brings a hint of colourful fun.
