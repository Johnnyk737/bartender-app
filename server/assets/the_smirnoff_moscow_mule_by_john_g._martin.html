The Smirnoff Moscow Mule by John G. Martin

210 2.5
Easy

This famous combination of clean, pure Smirnoff No. 21 Vodka, spice and citrus zest is one of the classics. Created by John G Martin and his bar owner friend Jack Morgan, it sparked the cocktail revolution in the 1950’s.

About this recipe

John and Jack combined the fiery flavours of ginger beer, lime juice, bitters and Smirnoff to make a perfectly blended cocktail. Never one to settle for the ordinary, he gave it the perfect finish, serving it up in the now legendary copper cup.

How to make

  1. Fill a glass or copper cup with ice.

    Fill a highball glass or copper cup with ice cubes.

  2. Add Smirnoff No. 21 Vodka, lime juice, ginger beer and a dash of bitters.

    Using a jigger, measure 50ml Smirnoff No. 21 Vodka, 17.5ml lime juice, 120ml ginger beer and one dash of bitters into the glass.

  3. Stir thoroughly.

    Stir the mixture until well combined.

  4. Garnish with a wedge of lime.

    With a sharp knife and cutting board, slice a wedge of lime and place in the drink to garnish.
