Captain Morgan Original Spiced Gold & Ginger Ale

694 3
Easy

A double dose of delicious spice that fills the palate, with sweet caramel and vanilla flavours in Captain Morgan Original Spiced Gold balanced by a pour of dry ginger ale.

How to make

  1. Fill a glass with ice.

    Fill a tall glass with ice cubes.

  2. Pour Captain Morgan Original Spiced Gold and ginger ale into the glass.

    Using a jigger, measure 50ml Captain Morgan Original Spiced Gold and 125ml ginger ale into the glass.

  3. Garnish with a wedge of lime.

    Using a sharp knife and chopping board, cut a slice of lime and place on top of the drink to garnish.
