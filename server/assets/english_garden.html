English Garden

228 2.5
Medium

This is the patio cocktail par excellence, a pairing of cucumber, apple and mint that’s fresh from an English garden and delivers a tart mix of apple, fresh cucumber and tasty mint.

About this recipe

Taking inspiration from the ingredients that you can find in an English Garden, this fabulous new cocktail has classic Gordon’s as its base – delicious Crisp Cucumber Gin – that’s perfect with sweet and tart apple juice lifted by the aroma of mint and a slice of cucumber. Enjoy in an English garden – or imagine that you’re part of a perfect horticultural setting, glass of English Garden in hand.

How to make

  1. Fill a shaker with ice.

    Fill a cocktail shaker with ice cubes.

  2. Add Gordon’s Crisp Cucumber, apple juice, lime juice and sugar syrup to the shaker.

    Using a jigger, measure 25ml Gordon’s Crisp Cucumber, 50ml apple juice, 20ml lime juice and 20ml sugar syrup into the shaker.

  3. Shake until cold.

    Shake the mixture vigorously until the surface of the shaker feels chilled.

  4. Strain into a martini glass.

    Using a cocktail strainer, strain the mixture into a martini glass.

  5. Garnish with a slice of cucumber and mint leaf.

    With a sharp knife and a chopping board, cut a slice of cucumber and place on top of the drink to garnish, along with a mint leaf.
