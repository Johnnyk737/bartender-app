Perfect Scotch Sour

518 2
Medium

This version of the classic cocktail uses the more subtle flavours of Johnnie Walker Red Label Blended Scotch Whisky. It's just made for mixing, along with the sweet and slightly sour touch of the added lemon juice.

How to make

  1. Fill a shaker with ice.

    Fill a cocktail shaker with ice cubes.

  2. Pour Johnnie Walker Red Label Blended Scotch Whisky, lemon juice, sugar syrup, angostura bitters and egg white into the shaker.

    Using a jigger, measure 50ml Johnnie Walker Red Label Blended Scotch Whisky, 25ml lemon juice, 17.5ml sugar syrup and a dash of angostura bitters into the shaker. Add 1 egg white.

  3. Shake until cold.

    Shake the mixture vigorously until the surface of the cocktail shaker feels chilled.

  4. Strain into a glass.

    Using a cocktail strainer, strain the cocktail into a short glass.

  5. Garnish with a wedge of lemon.

    Use a sharp knife to cut a wedge of lemon on a chopping board and place on top of the drink to garnish.

Grab a bite

Get some grub in before or while you’re drinking – it slows alcohol absorption.
