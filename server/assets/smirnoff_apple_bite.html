Smirnoff Apple Bite

226 3
Easy

A simple pour of apple juice and lemonade adds tartness to the clean, clear flavour of Smirnoff. Tangy lemon adds a citrus lift, and it can be served on ice.

Ingredients for

◄
1
▸
  • 50ml.
    Smirnoff No. 21® Vodka
  • 100ml.
    Lemonade
  • 100ml.
    Apple Juice
  • 1wedge(s)
    Lime
  • ➤ 1.9 units of alcohol per serve

Equipment

  • 1 x
    Tall glass
  • 1 x
    Ice
  • 1 x
    Jigger
  • 1 x
    Knife
  • 1 x
    Chopping Board

How to make

  1. Fill a highball glass completely with cubed ice.

  2. Add Smirnoff and equal parts lemonade and apple juice.

  3. Squeeze in a lime wedge and drop in the drink for garnish.
