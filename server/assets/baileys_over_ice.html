Baileys Over Ice

212 2.5
Easy

A simply satisfying drink that can be enjoyed by anyone, anytime. Smooth sweet Baileys poured over ice satisfies as soon as it touches your lips.

    Baileys® Original Irish Cream Liqueur

Equipment

  • 1 x

  • 1 x

How to make

  1. Fill a glass with ice.

    Fill a short glass with ice cubes.

  2. Pour Baileys Original Irish Cream into the glass.

    Using a jigger, measure 50ml Baileys Original Irish Cream into a short glass.

Grab a bite

Get some grub in before or while you’re drinking – it slows alcohol absorption.

Baileys® Original Irish Cream

Since its creation in 1974, Baileys Original Irish Cream Liqueur, a delicious blend of quality whiskey and smooth Irish cream, has become one of the world’s best-loved liqueurs.
