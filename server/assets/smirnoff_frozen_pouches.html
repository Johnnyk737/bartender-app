We’re freezing Smirnoff!! Introducing Smirnoff Frozen Pouches. A delicious new drinking sensation from the number 1 spirits brand in the world. Enjoy refreshing fruit flavours combined with the smooth taste of Smirnoff Vodka straight from your freezer. Smirnoff Frozen Pouches are expertly made to be perfectly delicious every time – simply freeze, squeeze and enjoy.

How to Enjoy

Freeze, squeeze and drink. These are the three steps to a great serve for any of the Sminroff frozen range. These simple to pour pouches are made to be easily sampled and enjoyed. First you place them in the freezer at least eight hours before serving. Then, when you want to share with friends or enjoy yourself, take the pouch out and squeeze until you feel it turning to slush (if it’s really hard just run it under warm water until you feel it start to break up). Finally, pour the pouch into a glass while they’re still partly frozen and share.

Flavours

The new range, uses the classic Smirnoff 21 as a base and includes a range of fabulous flavours to suit even the most discerning taste buds: • Summer lovers with fruitier tastes can take in the sunset whilst sipping on the delicious new Smirnoff Sorbets, available in Lemon, Raspberry and Mango. • Smirnoff Apple Bite and Smirnoff Ice are great solutions for those looking to enjoy a classic vodka experience whilst relaxing in the garden, or hosting family and friends.

The Range

The new range provides a quick, easy and delicious way to enjoy frozen drinks this summer. Simply pop them in the freezer and squeeze into a glass when you’re ready to go, it couldn’t be easier! And the delicious range of flavours mean that there is sure to be something to suit all tastes. So whether you’re enjoying a family barbecue, a summer evening with friends or you just fancy a frozen treat on a hot summer’s day, Smirnoff’s new frozen range are your new go-to this season. All you need to do is freeze, squeeze and enjoy!
